A premium detailing studio in a category where most competitors look like
last-decade auto-shops. The brief was simple: make the work feel as
considered as the cars going through the bay.

<Pull>
The client doesn't read like a small business — and neither should the website that sells them.
</Pull>

## Approach

We built the identity around a single editorial idea: detailing as ritual. The
type system reads like a magazine, the photography is shot like still-life,
and the booking flow is engineered for trust over volume.

<FullBleed caption="Hero treatment — booking-first, image-led" />

## Outcome

Inbound rose roughly 3x in the first quarter post-launch. Average ticket size
grew alongside it — the new positioning attracted clients who came for the
detail and stayed for the relationship.

<Spread caption="Booking surfaces — desktop and mobile" />

The site became the studio's strongest sales asset. It now sets the tone for
every conversation that follows.
